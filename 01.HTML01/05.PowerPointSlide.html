Heading and Paragraphs

  • Heading Tags: <h1> - <h6>

      <h1>Heading 1</h1>
      <h2>Sub heading 2</h2>
      <h2>Sub heading 3</h3>
  • Paragraph Tags <p>

      <p>This is my first paragraph</p>
      <p>This is my second paragraph</p>
  • Sections: <div> and <span>

      <div>div style="background: skyblue;">
      <div>This is a div</div>
      </div>
